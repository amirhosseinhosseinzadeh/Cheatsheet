* => not exist or exist many
+ => one or more exists
? => not exist or exist one 
\d => decimal digit
\D => non digit characters
\s => white space characters
\S => non white spaces
\w => words alpha numerics
\W => non word non alpha numericals
^ => at first of the line 
$ => at end of the line
[] => compare with values exist in brackets
{4} => Count 4 o f the specific char 
{1,4} => atleast 1 and at max 4 of specic char
